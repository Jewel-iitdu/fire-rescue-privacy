Privacy Policy

Fire Safety Management Gaming · Effective date: 11 June 2026

This privacy policy describes how Fire Safety Management Gaming ("the app", "the game"), developed by Jewel Rana, handles information when you use it on your device.

The short version: Fire Safety Management Gaming is a fully offline game. It does not collect, store, transmit, share or sell any personal information — from anyone.

1. Information we collect

None. The app does not collect any personal data, usage data, device identifiers, location data, contacts, photos, files, or any other information. It does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any details about yourself.

2. Internet access and third parties

The game is played entirely offline. It contains no advertising, no analytics or tracking SDKs, no in-app purchases, and no social-media integrations. No data is sent to us or to any third party.

3. Permissions

The app does not request any sensitive device permissions (such as camera, microphone, location, or storage access beyond its own installation).

4. Children's privacy

Fire Safety Management Gaming is an educational disaster-preparedness game suitable for general audiences. Because the app collects no information whatsoever, it also collects no information from children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations by design — there is simply nothing collected.

5. Data security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk. Any progress the game saves (such as settings or mission results) is stored locally on your device only, and is removed when you uninstall the app.

6. Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes how data is handled (for example, by adding online features), this policy will be updated first and the effective date above revised. Material changes will be highlighted in the app's release notes.
